During the Workout: The workout at St. Ignatius is a classic example of a workout with varying interval length as well as different athletes completing different workouts. The workout is designed to be a 2-3 x 1 mile workout with 60 seconds rest between miles, then two hard 800-meter efforts at the finish with 3 minutes rest.

After the Workout: The Live Graph allows the coach to gain some critical insights into his athlete's performance without even looking at numerical data. They will be able to evaluate the amount of rest each athlete is taking relative to other athletes, and notice how even the athletes are running.

By switching to the Live Results tab the visual observations made through viewing the graph can be checked by reading from the values on this list. Rest intervals can be checked to see if athletes are taking the most beneficial amount of rest. The interval lap times can be checked for consistency as well as intensity.
