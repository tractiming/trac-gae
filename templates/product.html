Product

Our Software: Designed to scale for any race. The software can be run through your smartphone app or via web browser. Push live text updates or send emails when a friend of family member passes the finish. Finally, review results and compare times too.

Race Day Information:

Durable Chips are worn on foot and are designed for practices. Disposable chips are sent on a racing bib.
System Rentals:
Mass start & finish line (>15ft): 1
Mass start & wide finish line (<15ft): 2
If you intend to have a chip timed start, that is not the finish, add 1 (>15ft) or 2 (<15ft)
For checkpoints, depending on the width, add the necessary number of systems. If races are circular, and start and finish in the same place, the same systems can be used.
