About

To the backdrop of Northwestern University, Elliot and Griffin met their freshman year running track & field. They have built a strong sense of collaboration, not only as teammates on the track, but through working on hours of engineering homework together.




After finishing their master's degrees in the winter of 2013, Elliot and Griffin thought it would be the end of their journey. However, after forgetting his watch multiple times on lonely winter runs, Griffin realized technology was missing from track and field and cross-country running; technology that was simple, easy to use, and available on demand. Needing a partner who could share the dream.




TRAC was born.
